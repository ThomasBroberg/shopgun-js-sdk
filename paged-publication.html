Make your PDF alive with ShopGun.

ShopGun has developed a new JavaScript SDK that allows you to integrate your PDF publication directly on your website without any use of iframes. It's a total game changer. In fact, this page is powered by the new SDK to show its power. Give it a try by using your arrow keys or swiping.

Highlights. There are a lot of them.

Upload once. Distribute everywhere.

Using the ShopGun CMS you simply have to upload your PDF in a single place. Then ShopGun takes care of distributing the PDF in our own apps and website + your apps and website if you are using our SDK.

Real-time analytics.

When the PDF gets published we automatically start tracking key metrics in the publication such as opens, pageviews, offer clicks. In real-time. We can also make custom reports showing you activity grouped by zipcode and normalized by population showing you the true value of each zipcode. You can even integrate your own analytics platform on top of our SDK such as Google Analytics.

Automatic offer extraction.

We've developed our own proprietary service that automatically extracts all the offers from your PDF. This adds interactivity to the reading experience allowing people to click each offer and add it to your shopping list/basket, see more details etc.

Customizable and extensible.

As the first company in the world, we've developed a solution that allows you to integrate your PDF directly on your website without the use of iframes. This doesn't just make the experience faster but it allows you to customize everything since the content isn't in a walled garden. We believe in you having the choice. Our SDK is even open-source allowing you to modify its behavior.

Embed custom HTML.

You can now embed custom HTML directly in the reading experience. For example, make an intro or outro where you include a newsletter form, a link to download your app or something else. And all the HTML will use your font family, font size, colors etc. because we don't use iframes.

SEO juice.

Because the SDK doesn't use iframes, it also means Google and other search engines can see the contents of your PDF. People should find your website when searching for you - not a 3rd party provider that displays your PDF. It will grow your organic traffic and allow you to pitch other features on your website.

Getting started.

If you're interested in using our new SDK on your website (and/or apps) email us at business@shopgun.com and we'll get in touch. For more in-depth technical information check out our GitHub repo.
